About Our Team

Ron Hennig

Founder & Lead Accountant

Based in Lawrence Township, NJ, I bring a structured, compliance-focused approach to tax, bookkeeping, and payroll for individuals and small businesses. My professional background includes auditing public and private companies across manufacturing, software, real estate, and financial services.

Earlier in my career, I worked with a tax industry financial institution conducting compliance audits of tax preparers and supporting regulatory responses at the federal level. That experience shaped how I approach client work today — with a strong focus on accuracy, documentation, and defensible financial records.

When I handle your books or taxes, the goal isn’t just to get things done — it’s to create clean, organized, and compliant financial records that support your business with confidence.
